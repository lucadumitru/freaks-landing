FAQ

Q1: As a DJ, I'm interested in playing at the festival. How can I get involved

A1: Calling all DJs! We're excited to have you on board! To give talented DJs an incredible opportunity to play at Freaks of Nature, we'll be hosting an online DJ competition. Keep a close eye on our Instagram account for the announcement, and get ready to showcase your skills and set the stage on fire with your electrifying beats! Let's make the crowd dance like never before!

Q2: When will the festival timetable be announced?

A2: The timetable will be dropped in the week leading up to the event. Stay tuned to our socials for updates, and we'll keep our freaks family entertained with all the latest news and important information. Have a freaktastic day, and we can't wait to host you! 🙂🎉

Q3: Interested in marketing or media partnerships to promote the festival?

A3: Hey there, party people! 🎉 We're grateful for your love and support! If you're interested in collaborating with us on marketing or media partnerships, let's make something magical happen together! Reach out to us at, and let's take our creativity to new heights! 🚀

Q4: Where is the festival located?

A4: Wondering where the freaks are at? Find us at goo.gl/maps/ajWmSfQV8jt4a2VW9. Follow the beat, and you'll arrive at the heart of our vibrant celebration! 🗺️🌟

Q5: Interested in VIP tables and boxes?

A5: Elevate your festival experience with our exclusive VVIP tables and boxes! For inquiries and bookings, please email vip@freaksofnature.me . Let's make your time at Freaks of Nature extra special! 🎊🍾

Q6: I am a visual artist/clothing designer interested in showcasing my talent at the festival?

A6: Calling all visual artists and clothing designers! This is your chance to shine! Join the coolest boutique festival in Saudi and share your creativity with our free-spirited family. Visual artists and clothing designers can exhibit free of charge. Just send an email to masha@disruptgroup.me and show us what you've got! We can't wait to see your incredible creations! 🎨👗

Q7: What's the dress code for the festival?

A7: Express yourself freely! Embrace your inner dancing diva or disco dude. Feel the magic with sequins and glitter – they add sparkle to our vibrant celebration! Let your style shine bright and be part of the colorful tapestry that is Freaks of Nature! ✨

Q8: What is the lineup for the festival?

A8: Discover the hottest lineup in town, featuring a sensational mix of international and local DJs! Visit our website for the full list of sizzling artists. 🎵🔥

Q9: Are there any age restrictions for the festival?

A9: This is an 18+ event, but don't worry, everyone's welcome to unleash their inner party animal! Gather your adult friends and family, and let's spread good vibes together! 🎉🎈

Q10: Will there be parking available?

A10: Absolutely! Parking spots for your four-wheeled party chariots await you. Follow the beats and look for the giant disco ball guiding you to parking heaven. Want a premium on-premises parking experience? Act fast, as spots are limited! ⚡🚗

Q11: Can I bring my own food and drinks?

A11: Certainly! Indulge in our amazing food area that'll make your taste buds dance, or reserve a spot at our VIP gastronomic heaven on our website. Just remember not to bring any glass items. Let's feast, toast, and leave the glass at home! 🏠🚫🍷

Q12: What happens if it rains during the festival?

A12: Rain or shine, the show must go on! We've got a rainproof dance floor ready for you. Bring your funky rain gear if the forecast calls for it. A little water never hurt anybody, right? ☔🌦️

Q13: Can I get a refund if I can't attend the festival?

A13: We understand, but unfortunately, our refund policy is strict. Once purchased, tickets are non-refundable. Consider passing them on to a lucky friend! 🎫🍀

Q14: Can I buy tickets at the entrance?

A14: We recommend securing your tickets in advance as they sell quickly. Limited tickets may be available at the entrance if you're feeling lucky. Fingers crossed, my friend! 🤞🎟️

Q15: Will there be a lost and found area at the festival?

A15: We've got you covered! Our dedicated lost and found area will take care of misplaced items. Remember to keep an eye on your belongings and avoid testing the memory! 🕵️‍♀️🧳
